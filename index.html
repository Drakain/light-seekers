What we do

Experience

We bring you to some of the most beautiful places that the northern parts of Scandinavia has to offer. Experience the beauty of nature and be inspired.

Travel

Transportation is taken care of by us. Depending on your preferred mode of transportation we will arrange an exciting mix of ways to travel. The journey is as much a part of the experience as the destination.

Enjoy

Lodging will be arranged in some of our own cottages throughout Sweden, Norway and Finland. They are simple, but we guarantee comfort, warmth and amiable company.
